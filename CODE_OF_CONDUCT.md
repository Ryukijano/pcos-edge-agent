# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, personal attacks, and discrimination. Report issues to the maintainers. Violators may be warned, blocked, or banned.
